FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS WHALEX PROTOCOL?+
WhaleX is a Layer-1 protocol built on Solana, designed to eliminate the gap between retail traders and Whales. It provides institutional-grade tools, lightning-fast execution, automated security filtering, and a comprehensive investor dashboard with rank tracking and claim system.
WHAT IS $WHX TOKEN?+
$WHX is the native utility and governance token of the WhaleX ecosystem. Used for trading fee discounts, governance voting, staking rewards, and rank qualification. 70% of all protocol fees fund Buyback & Burn, creating continuous deflationary pressure.
WHICH BLOCKCHAIN IS WHALEX BUILT ON?+
WhaleX is built natively on Solana as an SPL Token — chosen for its sub-second execution speed, near-zero transaction fees, and 65,000+ TPS capacity. This ensures the fastest possible professional trading experience on-chain.
WHEN CAN I CLAIM MY $WHX TOKENS?+
Tokens are claimable at the Token Generation Event (TGE). When the presale concludes, reconnect your wallet to the dashboard and click Claim. Staking rewards and purchased tokens become available simultaneously. Your wallet is tracked in the database from purchase.
WHAT INVESTOR RANKS ARE AVAILABLE?+
WhaleX has three presale investor ranks: Top VIP (Stage 1 — earliest access, lowest price), VIP (Stage 2), and Gold (Stage 3). Each rank provides progressively different staking multipliers and early access benefits. Ranks are permanently recorded in the database.
HAS THE WHALEX CONTRACT BEEN AUDITED?+
Yes. The WhaleX smart contract undergoes multi-layer security auditing before Mainnet launch. All audit reports are publicly disclosed and verifiable. The automated security filtering system also monitors every transaction in real-time post-launch.
